About the Project

The IoT Gas Sensor Array provides an innovative solution for monitoring cooking activities in a non-intrusive manner. By leveraging cutting-edge gas sensors, this system can detect cooking fumes and hazardous gases in real-time, ensuring a safer and more efficient cooking environment. With the use of blockchain technology, the data gathered by the sensor array can be securely stored, providing accurate tracking and enhanced safety for the kitchen.

Features

Real-Time Gas Detection

Detects gas levels in real time, ensuring your cooking environment is safe from harmful fumes and gases.

Non-Intrusive Tracking

The system works unobtrusively, providing accurate data without disturbing your daily cooking routine.

Blockchain Data Security

Data gathered from the sensors is securely stored on the blockchain, ensuring integrity and privacy.

Smart Alerts

Users will receive smart alerts in case of dangerous gas leaks, improving kitchen safety and responsiveness.

Energy Efficient

Designed with energy efficiency in mind, ensuring minimal power consumption while maximizing performance.

Easy Integration

Easily integrates with existing smart home systems for seamless operation and enhanced functionality.
